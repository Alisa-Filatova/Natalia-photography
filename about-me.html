Hello, I am Natalia! Welcome to my world!!

I am a creative newborn, wedding and family portrait photographer based in a beautiful nature in California that enjoys working at home and (well who wouldn't) away!
I have shot weddings and other events all over the California.

I am a happy, creative person that loves to make beautiful, natural, soft, romantic and thoughtful images that reflect my artistic style.
I am a wife to one of americans finest, a mum to a two beautiful girls who is a sucker for a good love story, (I have been known to cry at many films... and TV adverts!!! You may even notice I often shed a few tears behind my camera when capturing people in love!)
I love a happy home full of flowers, candles, laughter, life and lots of music.

After working hard to get better and better with my camera, I decided to turn my passion of photography into a business. There is nothing more satisfying to me than spending a day with my husband, family and friends BUT spending a day behind the camera... well, that's a very close second! I never get tired of capturing a family's love or hearing the laughs of children after they are done with a silly pose, or the look I see in a client's face after handing over their images. I hope that one day soon, I will be able to bring my love of photos into your home that will fill you with tiny, captured memories that will last a lifetime...

Please feel free to look through my portfolio for a taste of my work. If you are interested in booking a shoot or purchasing a gift voucher as a gift, please just drop me a message on my Contact page.
